<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">林景华文小学堂</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;">林景小学的华文旗舰课程“林景华文小学堂”，为学生提供全面、沉浸式的学习体验，培养学生对语言、文化的兴趣，帮助他们更好地掌握华文、华语</p>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family:KaiTi; "> “林景华文小学堂”让学生有机会接触多元化的学习活动，包括文化鉴赏、戏剧及翻译等。在疫情期间，学校也与中国的友校开展线上浸濡活动，这一活动受到了学生、家长的广泛欢迎。通过旗舰课程，校方提高了学生的学习兴趣和语文能力。</p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;">Woodgrove Primary School has implemented the Chinese Language (CL) Flagship Programme to strengthen students’ CL mastery and nurture interest in CL language and culture. The programme aims to provide holistic and immersive learning experiences for all students.</p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;">In this programme, students are exposed to a plethora of activities in spiral progression, such as cultural appreciation activities, drama workshops and translation lessons. During the pandemic, the school conducted a virtual immersion programme with a school in China, which was well-received by both students and parents.</p>
